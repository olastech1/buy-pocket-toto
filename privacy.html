Privacy Policy

At Pocket Toto, your privacy and discretion are fundamental to our business. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order and providing customer support.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to process your Payment on Delivery order, verify your delivery details, and communicate with you regarding your purchase. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties, marketing agencies, or data brokers.

3. Data Security

We implement strict internal security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access. Once your order has been successfully delivered and finalized, your delivery details are archived securely.

4. Discreet Communication

Any calls or emails regarding your order will be conducted with the utmost discretion to protect your privacy. Our representatives will never explicitly state the nature of the product when calling to confirm your address; we simply refer to it as "your package".
