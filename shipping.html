Shipping Policy

At Pocket Toto, your privacy and satisfaction are our top priorities. We offer fast, reliable, and completely discreet shipping nationwide across Nigeria.

1. Discreet Packaging

All orders are shipped in plain, unmarked packaging. There are no logos, product descriptions, or brand names on the outside of the box or dispatch envelope. Your delivery driver will not know the contents of your package.

2. Delivery Timelines

  • Lagos: Next-day delivery for orders confirmed before 2 PM.
  • Outside Lagos: 2–5 business days depending on your exact location in Nigeria.

3. Payment on Delivery

We offer Payment on Delivery (POD) nationwide. After placing your order on our website, a member of our team will call you to confirm your address. Please ensure the phone number provided is reachable. Your order will only be dispatched after verbal confirmation.

4. Delivery Costs

We currently offer FREE nationwide delivery on all Pocket Toto orders within Nigeria as a special promotion.

5. Failed Deliveries

If you are unavailable to receive your package, the courier will attempt delivery again. After multiple failed attempts or if you refuse to pay upon delivery without a valid reason, the package will be returned to us, and you may be restricted from future Payment on Delivery privileges.
